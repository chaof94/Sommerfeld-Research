The effective permittivity of a 2DEG interms of its adjacent layers can be expressed as:

eps_eff(k) = [eps_2 + eps_1 *coth (kd)/2]/2

where eps_1 and eps_2 are the relative permittivities of the lower and upper layers respectively. 
d is the distance from the gate
k is the wavenumber
